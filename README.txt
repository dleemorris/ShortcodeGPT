ShortcodeGPT is a powerful WordPress plugin that helps you create high-quality, engaging content with ease. With its intuitive interface and powerful AI-driven tools, ShortcodeGPT makes it easy to generate catchy captions, compelling product descriptions, and attention-grabbing headlines that drive clicks and conversions.

== Description ==
ShortcodeGPT is the ultimate content creation tool for website developers, content creators, and small business owners. Our plugin is designed to help you save time and effort by automating the content creation process, so you can focus on what matters most - growing your business.

With ShortcodeGPT, you can:

Generate catchy captions, compelling product descriptions, and attention-grabbing headlines
Optimize your content for SEO and drive more traffic to your site
Create engaging social media content that attracts and retains your audience
Craft subject lines and email content that increase open rates and conversions
Choose from three different service plans tailored to your content creation needs
ShortcodeGPT is easy to use, even if you have no prior experience with AI or automation tools. Our plugin includes comprehensive documentation and responsive support to help you get the most out of your investment.

== Installation ==

Upload the shortcodegpt folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
Use the ShortcodeGPT interface to generate high-quality content effortlessly
